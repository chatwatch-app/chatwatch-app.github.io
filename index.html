ChatWatch

Live-stream chat on your wrist.

ChatWatch puts your Kick, Twitch, and YouTube Live chat on your Wear OS smartwatch — built first for streamers who want to read their own chat between game inputs without ALT-tabbing off the stream.

What you get

  • •Live chat on the watch for Kick, Twitch & YouTube Live
  • •Distinct haptic patterns for donations, subs, gift subs, Super Chats, and raids — feel what just happened without looking
  • •Visual highlight on the watch when your name appears in chat
  • •Tile + complication for at-a-glance channel status
  • •Standalone mode — watch connects directly without the phone in range
